Community Support

Tile5 is an Open Source product, licensed under the liberal MIT-licence to ensure maximum flexibility. As such, building and supporting a support community is our primary goal. Sidelab Australia is committing one full-time resource (plus one part-time) to monitor community feedback and help that grow.

Free support will be offered via the mailing list - check out the information on the community page for further details.

Custom Development Services

In addition to support services, Sidelab is looking to partner with forward thinking companies to integrate Tile5's open mapping (and more) into their products and/or organisation. Tile5 (amongst other products) opens up a myriad of opportunities for both to leverage OpenSource mapping to create competitive advantage or reduce current spend around mapping technologies.

For more information, please contact info -at- sidelab.com
