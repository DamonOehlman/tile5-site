Tile5 is an opensource (MIT licensed) javascript library that provides a tile-based mapping solution designed to rival a native mobile mapping experience.

Enough - Show Me Demos

Cross Device Compatible

Our primary target was to provide a compelling experience for iPhone users, and we are currently working on Android support with the intention of supporting all "Class A" rated devices (as per the jQuery Mobile GBS Chart).

Device Support Details

Multiple Map Providers

Tile5 has been built from the ground-up to be a vendor neutral implementation. The cross-product of map providers and mobile devices is just crazy, and our goal was to make Tile5 "shine no matter what you're made of".
