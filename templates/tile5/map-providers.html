Other Map Providers

Sidelab are always happy to investigate implementing additional map providers based on client needs or provider sponsorship. Tile5 has been built from the ground up to support this so in general, this can be done with relative ease.

NOTE:

Additionally, while Tile5 allows you to mix and match provider functions, be aware that this may violate the provider terms of service.
